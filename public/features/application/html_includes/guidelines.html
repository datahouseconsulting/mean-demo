Aloha Researchers
Thank you for your interest in conducting research in the Hawaii Department of Education.
For security reasons, your session will expire after 90 minutes. It is strongly recommended that you save your application frequently using the “Save application without submitting” button at the bottom of each page to avoid losing any changes.
Please be aware that pop-up messages may appear during your session. For directions on what to do when these pop-up messages appear, please visit the FAQs page .
Directions on closing the application may also be found on the FAQs page .
Also, please remember to check the “User Profile” tab above to confirm that your contact information is up-to-date. All communication from HIDOE related to your application will be sent to the contact information listed there.
If you have any questions about the application, please email the Data Governance Office.
